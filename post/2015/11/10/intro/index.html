An Introduction

10 Nov, 2015 · by Nathan J · Read in about 1 min · (102 Words)

The first posts you’ll see on this site are titled «This week in the DBA universe».

Which is incorrect on a number of levels, not least:

  • They are collated somewhere between weekly and monthly
  • They focus on SQL Server and MySQL news, which are a very, very small subset of the DBA universe

However, it’s the best title I’ve got for now.

Pythian’s log buffer has a great set of curated content focused on database development and design, but I hope this is helpful for people who’d like to keep up to date with what’s new with database software and toolsets themselves.
